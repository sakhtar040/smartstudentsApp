We will not disclose customer name, addresses, or account information to unaffiliated parties, other than: o Where The Customer Requests Or Consents To Disclosure. o Where We Are Legally Required To Do So. o Where We Have Agreed To Provide Information Under Negotiated Contracts And Where The Customer Has Been Informed About The Possibility Of Sharing Information For Marketing Or Other Similar Purposes Through A Prior Direct Communication And The Customer Has Been Given The Reasonable Opportunity And Time To Decline.
